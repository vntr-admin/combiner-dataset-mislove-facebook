This trace set is a sample from the New Orleans Facebook network sampled by Mislove et al in January of 2009.  It has 63,731 vertices, 817,090 edges, an assortivity of 0.1769, and partition removals at 4134 and 5071.

It was provided by Alan Mislove.

Viswanath, Bimal, Alan Mislove, Meeyoung Cha, and Krishna P. Gummadi. "On the evolution of user interaction in facebook." In Proceedings of the 2nd ACM workshop on Online social networks, pp. 37-42. ACM, 2009.
